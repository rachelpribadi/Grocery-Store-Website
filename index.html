Crispy Sweet and Sour Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup sweet and sour sauce
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  2. Dip chicken in egg, then coat with cornstarch.
  3. Deep-fry chicken until golden and crispy.
  4. Stir-fry vegetables and pineapple briefly.
  5. Add sauce and chicken, toss well and serve.

Classic Assorted Sushi Platter

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 3 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Nori sheets
  • Assorted fillings (salmon, tuna, cucumber, avocado)

Instructions

  1. Cook rice and let cool slightly.
  2. Mix vinegar, sugar, and salt into rice.
  3. Spread rice over nori sheets.
  4. Add fillings and roll tightly.
  5. Slice and arrange on a platter.

Xiao Long Bao with Pork Filling

Ingredients

  • 250g ground pork
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp chopped spring onions
  • Dumpling wrappers
  • 1/2 cup pork gelatin stock (chilled)

Instructions

  1. Mix pork with soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, and spring onions.
  2. Fold in chilled gelatin cubes gently.
  3. Place filling into dumpling wrappers.
  4. Pleat and seal the edges.
  5. Steam for 8–10 minutes and serve hot.

Chinese Fried Rice with Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked jasmine rice (cold)
  • 200g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and cook shrimp until pink, then set aside.
  2. Stir-fry garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add eggs and scramble lightly.
  4. Add rice and vegetables, stir-fry well.
  5. Return shrimp, add soy sauce, toss and serve.
